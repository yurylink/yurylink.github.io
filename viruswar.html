Virus War

Defenders of the Body

The world is overtaken by a deadly virus and there is no much we cant do about it except stay home and stay safe but, in this game, you can have your revenge you can fight back . . .
by shooting them all!
Play as a Blood cell trying to defend yourself against three types of viruses: blue, red and green. Match the colour of you shot with the colour of the virus to kill them faster, or just shoot them 3 times, you call.
Let's see how much you can hold!


Green Virus

Fast and Deadly. Not going to fight back, but once spotted you, will rush nonstop.

Red Virus

Will circle you, and shoot you in your blind spot, be aware.

Blue Virus

Slow death. Dont move too much, take a while to start shooting, but once they start, will not stop.
